Blood cannot be made or manufactured outside of the body. Obviously, blood is necessary for life. ABOUT BLOOD

“Donate your blood for a reason, let the reason to be life” Use to raised awareness for a blood drive at Crowne Plaza, Kuala Lumpur, the quote reminds you that donating blood means supporting lifes. Simple, powerful, straight to the point, exactly the way slogan should be.

There are four major blood groups determined by the presence or absence of two antigens – A and B – on the surface of red blood cells. In addition to the A and B antigens, there is a protein called the Rh factor, which can be either present (+) or absent (–), creating the 8 most common blood types (A+, A-, B+, B-, O+, O-, AB+, AB-).
